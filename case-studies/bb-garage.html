DIESEL WORKSHOP · CASE STUDY

BB GARAGE

800%+ ROAS

Background

BB Garage is a diesel performance workshop that came to Mechanic Marketing looking to grow their Google Ads presence and generate more high-value jobs.

The Challenge

The workshop had previously run Google Ads without clear tracking or a dedicated landing page. They were getting clicks but couldn't measure what was converting — or why some months were quiet.

What We Did

We built a high-converting landing page, restructured their Google Ads campaigns around their best-performing services, and wired in full call and booking tracking. Every enquiry was attributed back to the ad that drove it.

The Results

BB Garage achieved over 800% ROAS — meaning for every dollar spent on ads, they returned more than $8 in revenue. They now have their best month, every month.
