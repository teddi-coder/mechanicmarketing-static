CORE DIESEL

2,147 LEADS

Background

Core Diesel is a high-performance diesel workshop that needed to scale its lead volume without sacrificing lead quality.

The Challenge

The workshop had strong word of mouth but wanted to grow beyond referrals. Previous digital marketing attempts hadn't delivered consistent results.

What We Did

A complete digital overhaul — new landing pages, restructured campaigns, and a full tracking setup that connected every enquiry to a revenue outcome. Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and GBP were all optimised together.

The Results

2,147 leads generated in 12 months — the highest lead volume of any MM client to date.
