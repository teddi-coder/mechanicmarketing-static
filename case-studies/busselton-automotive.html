Busselton Automotive Google Ads WA Ongoing

Introduction

Busselton Automotive Repairs is a trusted name in vehicle servicing across WA's southwest, particularly among drivers of large vehicles, 4x4s, and tradespeople. Yet despite their spotless shop floor and five-star reviews, their online reach wasn't keeping up.

Enter Mechanic Marketing, tasked with helping them connect their offline reputation to online lead generation.

Challenges

  • Generic online presence, not showcasing GVM or suspension specialisation
  • Paid ads too broad, lacking control over service-level focus
  • Underutilised social media, especially for trust-building
  • Rural audience underserved by city-centric digital strategies

Objectives

  • Build digital authority for suspension and GVM services
  • Increase qualified traffic from surrounding regions
  • Showcase the shop's friendly, welcoming culture
  • Improve tracking, ROI visibility and ad efficiency

Strategy

Mechanic Marketing developed a tailored digital strategy focused on relevance, reach, and relatability.

SEO: Authority with Rural Intent

To own their niche, we built a Topical Authority Map focused on:

  • High-value keywords like "GVM upgrades Busselton," "suspension lift kits WA," and "Dobinson suspension installers"
  • Service-specific landing pages for GVM upgrades, DOT inspections, and brand-specific suspension components (e.g. Dobinsons, EFS)
  • The GVM Upgrades page alone received over 1,460 impressions with notable traction from "Ford GVM upgrades" and "Chevy Silverado suspension" queries

Across the board, SEO delivered 258,000+ impressions and over 3,000 clicks — a 291% increase YoY.

Google Ads: Control by Category

We split campaigns into high-value focus areas:

  • Service-based campaigns (GVM upgrades, Suspension Kits, DOT Inspections)
  • Brand-specific ad groups targeting known keywords like "Dobinson GVM" and "EFS suspension problems"
  • Resulted in 430 conversions, a 13.7% conversion rate, and a CPA of just $12.22 — down 88% YoY

This gave the workshop dynamic budget control based on the season, staffing, or which services needed boosting.

Facebook Ads: Warm, Welcoming, and High-Impact

We leaned into the heart of Busselton Auto's brand — trust, warmth, and personality:

  • Retargeting ads using customer testimonials, bonus offers, and urgent call-to-actions
  • A "Welcome to the Workshop" ad series created a sense of community, particularly welcoming women into an industry where they're often underrepresented
  • "Meet Your Mechanic" videos showed real faces, real friendliness, and a real point of difference

Results included:

  • 61,000+ reach, with over 24,000 link clicks
  • A 543% increase in click-through rates (CTR)
  • Facebook conversions rose 200%, with the Personality and GVM ad sets performing strongest

Transparent Reporting & Attribution

Busselton Auto gained access to:

  • Real-time dashboards linking calls, bookings, form fills, chats and SMS replies
  • Granular data by campaign, service, channel and demographic
  • Clear ROI metrics that made campaign planning future-proof

Results

  • 430 total conversions from ads — up 2,766% YoY
  • 3,000+ SEO clicks and 258,000+ impressions in the last 12 months
  • Facebook engagement up 489%, driven by relatable video content
  • Email automation open rates above 50%, supporting ongoing customer relationships

From broad to brilliant, Busselton Automotive now has a system that earns trust, captures leads, and makes locals feel welcome before they even step inside.

Conclusion

Mechanic Marketing helped Busselton Automotive turn their shop's genuine culture into a powerful marketing advantage. With targeted SEO, precision Google Ads, and a Facebook presence full of personality, they're not just visible — they're the region's favourite mechanic.

Big upgrades, big reach, and big-hearted service — on repeat.
