SUSPENSION SPECIALIST · CASE STUDY

BUSSELTON AUTOMOTIVE

Under $30 CPL

Background

Busselton Automotive is a GVM and suspension specialist serving the South-West WA region.

The Challenge

The workshop was generating some enquiries but at a high cost per lead. Budget was being spent on broad targeting that wasn't bringing in the right jobs.

What We Did

We tightened the campaign targeting to focus on GVM upgrades, suspension work, and local searchers. Landing page copy was rewritten to speak directly to 4x4 and touring customers.

The Results

Cost per lead dropped to under $30 — a significant improvement that made every marketing dollar go further.
