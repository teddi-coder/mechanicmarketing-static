3 Scalable Marketing Tactics for Growing Auto Repair Chains

10 Aug 2025 · 8 min read

Growing from a single auto repair shop to multiple locations is a significant step. However, with growth comes complexity — especially in your marketing. What worked when you were a one-shop operation may not work across several sites. The key is finding marketing tactics that scale with you: repeatable, trackable, and flexible enough to adapt to each location's local market.

The first tactic is localised Google Ads by location. Running a single national campaign across multiple suburbs is wasteful. Each location should have its own campaign targeting its local area, its own landing page reflecting that suburb, and its own conversion tracking. This lets you see which locations are performing, where to increase budget, and where to investigate problems.

The second tactic is a unified Google Business Profile strategy. Each location needs its own GBP listing, actively managed with location-specific posts, photos, and review responses. A customer in Parramatta doesn't care about reviews from your Penrith location — they want to see that the shop down the road has a proven track record. Localised GBP listings significantly improve local map pack rankings for each site.

The third tactic is centralised reporting with location breakdowns. When you're operating multiple sites, you need a single dashboard that shows performance by location — leads, cost per lead, conversion rate, and revenue — so you can make resourcing decisions based on data, not gut feel. WhatConverts or a similar call tracking platform makes this achievable without significant technical overhead.
