Car Repair Advertising Ideas That Actually Bring in More Customers

30 Mar 2025 · 6 min read

You don't need a massive budget or a marketing degree to attract more cars into your shop. These car repair advertising strategies have delivered real results for workshops — and you can start using them today.

Facebook Ads with a Special Offer

Facebook is still one of the most effective and affordable ways to reach local car owners. One local mechanic ran a "Free Brake Inspection" ad targeting drivers within 10km — and it filled their bookings fast. The photo of worn brake pads grabbed attention, a "Book Now" button made it easy to act, and "Limited Spots Available" added urgency. Keep your message short, use urgency in your call to action, target nearby drivers using automotive interests, and test different images to see what gets more clicks.

Before-and-After Instagram Posts

Instagram is perfect for showing off your work. A cracked bumper or filthy engine made new again gets attention — people love transformations and stop to look. Post regularly with local hashtags, add a quick customer quote in the caption, and tag the location. Even 10 seconds of a repair in progress as a Reel can significantly expand your reach.

Google Ads for Urgent Repairs

When someone's car breaks down, they're not browsing — they're searching. Focus on high-intent keywords like "engine repair near me" or "same day brake repairs." Highlight a specific offer in your ad headline. Always send people to a page that matches the ad — don't use your homepage. A well-targeted Google Ad appearing at the right moment converts far better than any passive marketing channel.

Local Flyers with Neighbourhood Discounts

Flyers still work — especially in the right places. Print "$15 Off Any Service Over $100" offers and place them in nearby cafés, gyms, and laundromats. Use bold fonts, clear colours, and a QR code that links to your booking page. Physical marketing sticks around longer than digital — and in the right location, it reaches people you'd never find online.

Seasonal Promotions

Tie promotions to the calendar — pre-winter battery and tyre checks, pre-summer air conditioning service, pre-school-holidays road trip inspections. Seasonal relevance makes your offer feel timely and urgent rather than generic. Run these as Facebook ads, flyers, email campaigns, or Google Ads depending on your budget.

Video Testimonials

A 30-second video of a happy customer saying "these guys sorted my car out same day — highly recommend" is worth ten written reviews. Ask your best customers if they'd be willing to record a short clip. These videos work across Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, and your website. Authenticity converts far better than polished production.
