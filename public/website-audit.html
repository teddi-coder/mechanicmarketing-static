The 5 Mistakes · 01

The 5 mistakes killing your website

We audited 50 workshop websites across Australia. These five problems came up again and again.

01

One page trying to sell ten services

Google can't figure out what you actually do — so it shows someone else instead.

Dedicated page per service so Google knows what you do
02

No tracking on calls or forms

You're spending on ads but have no idea which ones actually bring in work.

Know exactly which ad brought each job
03

Painfully slow on mobile

If your site takes more than 3 seconds to load, half your visitors leave before it finishes.

Under 3 seconds or you lose half your visitors
04

No clear offer or next step

Visitors land on your site, look around, and leave without doing anything.

One CTA per page, make it obvious
05

Phone number buried or missing

People ready to book can't find how to call you — so they call the next shop.

Click-to-call, sticky, visible on every page
