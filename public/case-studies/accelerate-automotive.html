Accelerate Automotive Google Ads + SEO NSW Ongoing

Introduction

Accelerate Automotive is known for its top-tier service and expertise in European vehicles, but that same reputation wasn't translating into their digital presence. With inconsistent traffic, limited lead flow, and under-optimised ad spend, they turned to Mechanic Marketing to realign their online strategy and drive measurable growth.

Challenges

  • Generic and underperforming Google Ads that didn't reflect their specialisation
  • A flat SEO profile that failed to highlight their niche in European car servicing
  • Lack of strategic Facebook activity beyond basic promotions
  • Minimal clarity on ROI, making budget allocation guesswork

Objectives

  • Showcase expertise in European makes and models
  • Drive qualified leads from paid and organic search
  • Increase trust and engagement on social platforms
  • Establish consistent, data-backed performance reporting

Strategy

Mechanic Marketing rolled out a bespoke, high-precision digital marketing system, combining SEO foundations with ad innovation and compelling creative.

SEO: Topical Authority & Intent Matching

To grow search visibility where it mattered most, the team built a Topical Authority Map — mapping keywords that Accelerate could realistically dominate. This allowed us to:

  • Prioritise European vehicle makes and models, including high-intent terms like "Mercedes C-Class service Brisbane"
  • Create individual landing pages for each brand (Audi, BMW, Mercedes, etc.) to capture organic traffic
  • Build structured internal links to reinforce keyword hierarchy and support SEO growth

This content strategy resulted in a 347% increase in impressions and nearly 3,500 organic clicks in just 12 months.

Google Ads: Specialised Targeting, Dynamic Structure

Generic ads were replaced with high-converting, precision-built campaigns:

  • Individual campaigns and ad groups for each make/model, such as "BMW mechanic Brisbane" and "Audi service specialist"
  • Dynamic ad variants allowed the team to dial up or down spend per car type or service area
  • Result: A 374% increase in conversions and 157% decrease in CPA, with cost-per-lead dropping to just $13.00

Facebook Ads: Retargeting With a Human Touch

Mechanic Marketing brought trust-building and conversion-driving creativity to the table:

  • Retargeting Ads focused on urgency and action, offering limited-time bonuses, freebies with bookings, and customer testimonials
  • Launched "Meet Your Mechanic" videos introducing the real faces behind the business, breaking down barriers and humanising the brand
  • Delivered a 550% increase in Facebook leads, while reach and engagement continued to climb

Transparent, Actionable Reporting

Live dashboards tracked every click, call, and conversion across platforms. This empowered the Accelerate team to:

  • Identify high-performing campaigns in real-time
  • Shift budgets based on service demand (e.g. push Mercedes ads during peak months)
  • Make confident, data-led business decisions — month after month

Results

Accelerate Automotive has consistently hit record-breaking months with:

  • 3,700+ conversions from ads alone
  • Organic impressions surpassing 182,000 across targeted keywords
  • Facebook lead growth of over 550%
  • SEO click-throughs for brand and model queries like "Audi mechanic near me" and "European car service Brisbane"

What used to be seasonal peaks have become predictable, repeatable successes.

Conclusion

Mechanic Marketing helped Accelerate Automotive move from a generalist online presence to a specialist digital powerhouse. With SEO built around expertise, ads structured by intent, and social content grounded in trust, Accelerate now owns their niche — and converts it like clockwork.

It's not just a good month. It's their best month — every month.
