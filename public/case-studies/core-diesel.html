Core Diesel Google Ads + Landing Pages QLD 12 months

Introduction

Core Diesel Maintenance was already the go-to diesel tuning shop in Maryborough, but their online presence didn't reflect their industry leadership. They needed to better communicate their niche, drive high-intent traffic, and grow leads without waste.

That's where Mechanic Marketing came in, ready to build a digital engine that matched the strength of Core Diesel's workshop.

Challenges

  • Generic search presence not reflecting their specialisation in 4×4, tuning, or American trucks
  • Scattered Google Ads structure, missing out on high-intent queries
  • Little Facebook engagement beyond basic posts
  • No clear attribution of leads or ROI visibility

Objectives

  • Rank for diesel and 4×4-specific keywords across key regions
  • Attract leads for services like dyno tuning, ECU remapping, suspension upgrades
  • Rebuild trust and drive traffic via paid social
  • Deliver crystal-clear reporting on what's working — and why

Strategy

Mechanic Marketing developed a layered strategy focused on intent, authority and visual trust-building.

SEO: Precision Topics & Authority Mapping

The team constructed a Topical Authority Map, identifying high-opportunity keywords across diesel, 4×4, and American vehicle categories.

  • Created landing pages for specific services like dyno tuning, ECU remapping, and 4×4 suspension upgrades
  • Targeted keyword clusters like "diesel tuning near me," "Isuzu Hervey Bay service," and "truck shaker testing"
  • The /diesel-dyno-tuning/ page alone saw a 1,743% increase in traffic with a 61.6% engagement rate

SEO drove over 16,000 impressions and 500+ organic clicks, while high-performing content like "American trucks repair" and "4×4 repairs" cemented topical authority.

Google Ads: Service-Based Campaigns, Tuned to Convert

We overhauled Core Diesel's ads, replacing vague "mechanic near me" language with specific, high-converting campaigns:

  • Structured ads around core services: Dyno tuning, diesel repairs, ECU remaps, 4×4 upgrades, and American trucks
  • Developed individual ad groups so spend could be redirected in real-time based on seasonal demand or workshop priorities
  • Drove 362 tracked conversions, with an impressive 24% conversion rate and a CPA of just $17.32

Core's campaigns achieved a 4.45× ROAS, with high-value services like dyno tuning and ECU remaps performing strongest.

Facebook Ads: Trust, Offers, and Shareable Builds

Facebook and Instagram campaigns focused on trust and conversion psychology:

  • Retargeting creatives offered trigger-pulling messages — limited-time discounts, testimonial callouts, and service add-ons
  • "Meet Your Mechanic" ads brought the faces behind the wrenches into the spotlight
  • Highlighted visually appealing transformations of American trucks, using before-and-after imagery that naturally drove shares and interest

These efforts expanded reach and kept Core Diesel top of mind for customers circling the decision.

Reporting & Attribution

Mechanic Marketing provided a live dashboard that tracked:

  • Source-level conversion data (organic, paid, referral, social)
  • Performance per campaign, keyword, and ad group
  • High-performing pages by engagement and lead generation

This enabled the Core Diesel team to focus on what worked, fine-tune the strategy, and scale smart.

Results

  • 362 tracked conversions across calls, forms, and chats
  • Over 37,000 organic search impressions, with an average Google position of 11.2
  • 1,508 Google Ads clicks at a $4.16 average CPC
  • Facebook and landing page engagement spikes driven by /diesel-dyno-tuning/ and /american-trucks-repair/ content

The business saw record lead volume, with Google Ads, SEO, and Facebook all pulling their weight in the pipeline.

Conclusion

Core Diesel's digital presence now mirrors its workshop: high-performance, precision-built, and built to scale. With Mechanic Marketing's SEO structure, service-focused ads, and visual social content, they've engineered a marketing system as bulletproof as their builds.

More leads. More upgrades. More momentum. Month after month.
