FAQ

Common questions

What's the difference between Ignite and Accelerate?
Ignite covers Google Ads, Facebook Ads, a landing page, tracking, and reporting — everything you need to start generating leads. Accelerate adds SEO (blogs and on-page optimisation) and Google Business Profile management on top of everything in Ignite, giving you both paid and organic growth.
How soon will I see results?
Google Ads can drive traffic quickly, but profitable results take weeks to months as data is optimised. We typically see strong lead flow within the first 30 days, with costs improving every month.
How much should I spend on ads?
We tailor your ad budget based on your goals, location, and competition. Many workshops start with a few hundred dollars monthly and scale once they see results. Ad spend is separate from the management fee.
Will I be locked into a contract?
No. Our service is month-to-month. You can cancel at any time without penalty. We earn your business every month.
