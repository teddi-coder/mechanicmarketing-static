Google Ads can drive traffic quickly, but profitable results take weeks to months as data is optimised. We typically see strong lead flow within the first 30 days, with costs improving every month.
How much should I spend on ads?
We tailor your ad budget based on your goals, location and competition. Many workshops start with a few hundred dollars monthly and scale once they see results.
Will I be locked into a contract?
No. Both plans are month-to-month and you can pause or stop at any time. We earn your business by delivering results, not by forcing commitment.

Yes. Your ads, landing pages, tracking, and all your data are yours to keep — even if you leave. Full transparency and full ownership.
