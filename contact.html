FAQ

Common questions

How can digital marketing help my mechanic shop get more customers?
Digital marketing helps your mechanic shop by making sure people can find you online. Most customers search for mechanics on Google before deciding where to take their car. If your shop shows up in local searches and has good reviews, you're more likely to get picked. Having a clean, easy-to-use website and running Google Ads targeted to your area will bring in new customers.
I'm not tech-savvy. Can I still use digital marketing?
Absolutely. You don't need to be tech-savvy to benefit from digital marketing. That's exactly why we exist — we handle everything for you. You focus on fixing cars, we focus on getting them through the door.
Is digital marketing worth it for a small mechanic shop?
Yes. Traditional ads like flyers don't target customers as well as digital marketing does. With Google Ads, you can make sure your shop shows up when someone nearby searches for "mechanic near me." It might take a bit of time, but digital marketing can steadily bring in more business — especially when combined with quality service.
How do I get my shop to show up higher on Google?
Start by making sure your website is optimised with the right keywords — terms like "mechanic near me" or "car repair in [your city]." Set up your Google Business Profile, which helps you show up in local searches and on Google Maps. Encourage customers to leave reviews, and regularly update your site with fresh content.
What's the best way to target local customers?
Focus on Google Business Profile and local SEO for your suburb and surrounding areas. Google Ads with geo-targeting ensures your ads only show to nearby drivers. Facebook Ads with location radius targeting can also reach locals who haven't searched yet.
